History


Young and dynamic team of commercial and technical directors started working on this project of laying 68 KM long cross country pipeline from Bavda to Bakrol in 1999. We cross Sabarmati and Vatrak rivers, national highway, many irrigation canals, Indian railways and private farms. We paid cash compensation to the farmers against some damage to their crop. After lots of struggles and considerable delay in obtaining Western railway permission to cross Nadiad Bhadran railway line, we commissioned this gas line after necessary hydro test, lick test and nitrogen purging. We first time flare on 23-June-2001 at Bakrol Pressure Reducing Station Anand. With the support of very active industrialists and industrial association, knowing value and economy using natural gas started supporting Charotar Gas for installation of piped natural gas in their industries in GIDC and surrounding area. Also we started laying of pipe in downstream network and moving from street to street to convince domestic customers, the benefit and safety of using natural gas. Our first year turnover was Rs 1.08 Crores.

Details about CNG services...

CNG is compressed natural gas. Natural gas is mainly made up of methane and it is a very important fossil fuel because it is easy to transport through pipes. Natural gas is stored under high pressure as compressed natural gas (CNG). It produces non-toxic gases,so it is a cleaner fuel.



PNG

Details about PNG services...

Piped Natural Gas (PNG) is Eco-friendly fuel for Domestic Households i.e your Kitchens, Industries and Commercial Units. PNG is the convenient, consistent and safer source of fuel. It is supplied through pipeline and does not require the hassle for booking, handling, storing and measuring the underweight cylinder .
